## Intended use

- **Intended**: fine-tuning small models for instruction-following and
  runtime compliance under the WasmAgent training loop
- **Not intended**: general-purpose instruction tuning; open-domain
  chat; any use case without a governed sandbox execution environment

---

## Known limitations

- Small model (≤3B params) rollouts only; larger model behaviour untested
- IFEval task distribution — not representative of open-domain tasks
